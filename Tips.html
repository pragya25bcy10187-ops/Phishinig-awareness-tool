Safety Tips

Simple rule to avoid phishing

Do's and Don'ts

  • Never share OTP, PIN, CVV, passwords.

  • Check sender email carefully (spelling matters).

  • Don't click unknown shortened links (bit.ly,tinyurl).

  • Verify suspious messages from offical website only.

  • Enable 2FA (Two factor Authentication).

  • Report sacms to cybercrime portal.
